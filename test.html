About Me

I love working with my hands and ever since I picked up that random piece of scrap steel as a 12 year old and fashioned it into a knife, I was hooked. I am particularly drawn to the fabrication of high quality tools, the kind that lasts for generations. My goal is to create high quality knives that are well used, well loved, and well oiled. Thus allowing you and the generations to come to enjoy the pleasure of using a custom handmade blade.
